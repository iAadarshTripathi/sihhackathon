Background

Groundwater replenishment is critical for sustainable water resources. Rooftop rainwater harvesting (RTRWH) can augment supplies in both rural and urban areas. While CGWB provides technical manuals, people still lack a simple digital tool to assess their own potential.

Impact

Empower households and communities to take informed action. This tool boosts awareness, supports adoption of RTRWH & AR, and helps plan investments with indicative costs and benefits.
